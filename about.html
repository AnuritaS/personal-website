About

Anurita Sharma

Policy Strategist · Program Manager · Georgetown University

AS

I've spent nine years at the intersection of research and action — translating evidence into programs that serve real people. I started in county-level health administration, moved into federal evaluation work, and now work across the public and nonprofit sectors on policy strategy and program design.

I believe the best policy work is invisible — it just looks like things finally working. My approach is rigorous on the evidence side and deeply human on the implementation side.

At Georgetown I study public policy with a focus on program evaluation, causal inference, and applied data analytics — building the methodological depth to match the field experience.
